About

CINEHYD Film Festival is a platform for Hyderabad locals to showcase their talent in filmmaking and screenwriting. Our goal is to empower aspiring filmmakers and appreciate their immense talent. Beyond the competitive aspect, the festival serves as a hub for creative exchange, bringing together like-minded individuals who share a passion for storytelling. We wish to be as an important member in the Hyderbad film community by providing opportunities and connections to new up and coming filmmakers and ensure that they recieve the recognition they deserve.

To support student filmmakers, a 20% discount is offered for their films.

(Note: This festival is open to filmmakers/writers from states and countries as well)

Awards

  • Top 3 Winners from Each Category will Recieve a Certificate
  • Winner of 'Short Film' Category will recieve Funding for their Film and Consultancy Services
  • Selected Short Films and Feature Films will be Screened during the Festival
  • Honourable Mention Certificates to Exceptional Work that did not make it into Finalists
